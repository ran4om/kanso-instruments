# Kanso Instruments

## Register

Brand.

## Product purpose

Introduce Kanso Instruments, a fictional Berlin workshop building limited-run modular synthesizers, and turn curiosity into direct preorder enquiries. The page must feel like using an instrument, not reading a product brochure.

The fictional founder is Mina Okafor, a Berlin-based circuit designer and experimental percussionist.

## Audience

- Experimental musicians, sound artists, and composers who value tactile control.
- Small studios looking for distinctive, repairable hardware.
- Design-conscious collectors who want to understand the maker and manufacturing philosophy.

## Primary actions

1. Explore the Pulse/04 instrument through the interactive panel.
2. Read the product and workshop story.
3. Request an allocation by email.

## Brand voice

Restless, mechanical, playful. Short language, specific claims, no inflated futurism.

## Strategic principles

- Interaction demonstrates the product idea.
- Limited production is explained as workshop capacity, never artificial scarcity.
- The sound-reactive visual is illustrative and silent by default.
- Motion must have a complete reduced-motion alternative.

## Anti-references

- Black-and-neon cyberpunk synth clichés.
- Generic luxury product renders.
- Feature-card grids and vague innovation copy.

## Content constraints

No fake reviews, fake awards, or customer counts. No em dashes in public copy.
